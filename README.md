## Featured Project

### Microplastics In Australian Coastlines

Group data visualisation project for 41193 IS Visualization Studio.

The project investigated how microplastics in Australian coastal environments affect marine animals and Australians who consume local seafood. My individual section focused on the Australian Government as the stakeholder, with analysis designed to support policy decisions, intervention planning, monitoring priorities, and polymer regulation.
